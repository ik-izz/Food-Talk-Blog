Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients
•2 ounces of Spaghetti
•A few tablespoons of salt
•2 large eggs and 2 egg yolks
•1/2 ounces of grated Pecorino Romano
•1/2 ounces of grated Parmesan Cheese
•1/2 ounces of Guanciale diced into 1/4 inch cubes

    Steps

  • Step 1: Get a pot and place on the stove.

  • Step 2: Add water upto halfway and put on medium flame.

  • Step 3: Get a separate bowl and whisk the eggs and add salt, black pepper, and some pecorino to it.

  • Step 4: Let the water boil.

  • Step 5: Get a separate frying pan and cook the Guanciale cubes with or without oil until crisp and dark.

  • Step 6: Now add the Spaghetti to the boiling water and keep it until it is firm or al-dente.

  • Step 7: Keep 1 cup of the pasta water and drain the pot of the rest of the water.

  • Step 8: Reheat the Guanciale on the frying pan if necessary.

  • Step 9: Serve some of the pasta in a serving bowl and add the egg-cheese-salt-pepper mixture and transfer the Guanciale from the skillet to the serving bowl.

  • Step 10: Gently stir.

    • Useful Tips

  • Add as much salt as preferred for your tastes.

  • Use more egg yolks and less egg whites for a creamier flavor.
